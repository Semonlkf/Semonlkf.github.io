In traditional NLP,we regard words as discrete symbols.
Sunch symbols for words can be represented by on-hot vectors.

  • Distributional semantics:A word’s meaning is given by the words that frequently appear close-by

Word vectors

We will build a dense(稠密的) vector for each word,chosen so that it is similar to vectors of words that appear in similar contexts(语境).

Note:word vectors are also called word embeddings(词嵌入) or neural(神经的) word representations.They are a distributed representation.
